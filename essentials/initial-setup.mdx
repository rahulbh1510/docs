# Initial Setup/Onboarding

Welcome to AdoptAI! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get your account set up and ready to build your first AI agent.

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account
1. **Sign Up**: Visit the AdoptAI platform and create your account
2. **Email Verification**: Check your email and verify your account
3. **Organization Setup**: Create or join an organization workspace
4. **Profile Configuration**: Complete your user profile with relevant information

### Initial Configuration
- **Team Permissions**: Set up user roles and access levels
- **Billing Setup**: Configure your subscription and payment methods
- **Security Settings**: Enable two-factor authentication and other security measures

## Platform Overview

### Dashboard Navigation
- **Main Dashboard**: Overview of your agents and key metrics
- **Agent Builder**: Where you create and manage your AI agents
- **Analytics**: Performance insights and usage statistics
- **Settings**: Account and organization configuration

### Key Concepts Introduction
- **Agents**: Your AI assistants that interact with users
- **Actions**: Specific tasks your agents can perform
- **Tools**: Integrations and capabilities available to your agents
- **Playground**: Testing environment for agent development

## First Steps

### 1. Explore the Interface
Take a few minutes to familiarize yourself with the main navigation and available features.

### 2. Review Documentation
Browse through the key concepts to understand how AdoptAI works.

### 3. Check Examples
Look at sample agents and use cases to see what's possible.

### 4. Plan Your First Agent
Think about what you want your first agent to accomplish.

## Next Steps

Once you've completed the initial setup, you're ready to:
1. **Review Tools** available in the Agent Builder
2. **Explore Actions** that your agents can perform
3. **Build your first agent** using our guided process

The onboarding process is designed to get you productive quickly while ensuring you understand the platform's capabilities and best practices.
