# Custom Themes & Branding

Make your Copilot feel like a natural extension of your product by customizing its appearance to match your brand.

## What Can You Customize?

- **Colors:** Set your brand’s primary, secondary, and accent colors.
- **Logo:** Add your company’s logo for both light and dark modes.
- **Favicon:** Use your own favicon for browser tabs.
- **Typography:** (Coming soon) Choose custom fonts for a fully branded experience.

## How to Update Your Theme

All theming and branding options are managed in your `docs.json` configuration file.
